The Nritya Amrit - Initiative

I ' have founded Nritya Amrit, my initiative to use dance as a healing power for the in-house patients at wards of different hospitals and the underprivileged localities. Nrityaamrit is my initiative to blend the transformative power of classical dance with therapy, creating a unique approach to healing and empowerment. I use grace and rhythm of Kuchipudi to connect with patients, underprivileged communities, and diverse audiences, offering emotional well-being, cultural enrichment, and physical rejuvenation. My work spans hospitals, old age homes, and community venues. With a commitment to inclusivity, I also teach underprivileged youth, ensuring that the beauty of dance becomes a bridge for opportunity and hope.

Come join me in this Noble cause
