Unruly-Art

As a trained visual artist at the Delhi College of Art, my works have been exhibited at the renowned India Habitat Centre. I also associate with Unruly art, where I work with specially-abled children, who undergo vision correction surgeries ,and organise art exhibitions for them. I also work with the Project Prakash Foundation as a fundraiser to support the surgeries.
