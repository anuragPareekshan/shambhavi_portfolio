MUN (Modern United Nations)

Leadership amplified further when I became MUN Director General. The conference was my proving ground—hundreds of delegates debating global crises like space wars or technological ethics. My challenge was twofold: inspire collaboration and craft solutions under pressure.
I'd spend nights researching, blending scientific data (say, carbon emission stats) with diplomatic strategies. During debates, I mediated clashes, synthesized ideas, and steered committees toward resolutions.

Singapore United Nations Model

Shambhavi serves as the Director General for INTER SMUN, where she not only leads with vision but also empowers others with purpose. With a deep passion for diplomacy and global affairs, she plays a pivotal role in shaping the conference's strategic direction and ensuring that every committee runs with professionalism.

Strawberry Fields MUN – UNHCR

Shambhavi was selected to represent her school at the prestigious Strawberry Fields MUN 2023, where she served as a delegate in the UNHCR (United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees). Her selection reflected not only her strong understanding of international relations but also her ability to think critically and speak with empathy on global humanitarian issues.
