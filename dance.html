My Dance Profile

A trained Kuchipudi dancer , learning under the world-renowned Guru Raja Radha Reddy, I have performed in numerous prestigious shows including International Rajgir Mahotsav and the G20 Youth Connect programme , I currently lead Sanskriti's Cultural Council as President for the current session.

I tackled the challenge of uniting diverse student voices for events like festivals or fusion workshops. How do you honor tradition while appealing to modern tastes? I'd brainstorm with peers, propose science-art hybrids like a choreography rooted in physics and execute them. Over months, I developed a sixth sense for balancing creativity with feasibility, turning abstract ideas into celebrations that resonated. This role reinforced that problem-solving isn't solitary—it's about weaving collective strengths into solutions.
