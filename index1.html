Introduction

As 'a Class 12 science student, my life is a mix of learning, creativity, leadership, and new ideas. My journey began in the science classroom, where equations and experiments ignited my curiosity.

Physics, chemistry, mathematics, economics offered a lens to understand the world's mechanics, from the dance of subatomic particles to the complexity of ecosystems..

Science has taught me method—hypothesize, test, refine. Art has taught me intuition—trust the process, even when it's unclear. Leadership has taught me synthesis—blend perspectives for stronger outcomes. By Class 12, these threads wove into a talent I wield confidently, whether I'm solving a physics problem, curating an exhibit, or pitching clean tech.
